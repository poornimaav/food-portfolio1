Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa, also called Masale dosey ( ಮಸಾಲೆ ದೋಸೆ ), is a South Indian dish. It is a type of dosa and has its origin in the town of Udupi in Karnataka. It is made from rice, lentils, Urad dal, Chana dal, fenugreek, puffed rice, Toor dal, dry red chilli and served with potato curry, chutneys, and sambar. It is popular in South India, but can be found in all other parts of the country[5][6] and overseas.[7][8] In South India, the preparation of masala dosa varies from city to city. There are variations in Masala dosa like Davanagere butter dosa and paper masala dosa.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

2 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Masala Dosa breakfast food

instructions

step 1

Prepare Dosa Batter:Soak rice and urad dal in separate bowls for 4-6 hours and Strain.

step 2

Process rice and urad dal mixture in commercial grinder or food processor. While grinding, add water a cup at a time until it reaches the consistency of pancake batter.

step 3

Cover mixture and let sit overnight. Do not refrigerate; it needs to ferment.

ingredients

1-1/2 cups long grain rice

1/2 cup urad dal (polished black lentil or white lentil)

3 quarts water and Salt

Mixer

Grinder
